## 🏥Pharmacy Sales & Promotion Analytics Dashboard
🥈 Runner-Up – Data Analytics Challenge
📌 Project Overview

This repository contains my submission for the Pharmacy Sales & Profitability Analytics Challenge, where I was proud to be selected as a Runner-Up among several talented analysts.

The objective of this project was to transform a complex, enterprise-level pharmacy dataset into a decision-driven Power BI dashboard that delivers strategic insights across:

Revenue performance

Profitability drivers

Promotional impact

Product mix optimization

Store-level efficiency

Geographic revenue distribution

Rather than building a dashboard that only reports numbers, this solution was designed to answer key business questions and guide executive decision-making.

🎯 Business Objectives

This report was built to help stakeholders answer:

What is driving revenue growth?

Are margins improving or under pressure?

How effective are promotional campaigns?

Is revenue dependent on discounts?

Which product categories and pack sizes drive profitability?

How efficient are stores across regions?

Where are expansion opportunities?

📊 Dashboard Structure

The report is divided into three strategic pages, each serving a distinct analytical purpose.

🏢 Page 1: Enterprise Performance Overview
Purpose:

Provide a high-level executive summary of revenue, margin, and geographic contribution.

Key KPIs:

Total Revenue

Total Margin

Margin %

Average Revenue per Store

Total Units Sold

Key Insights:

104.6% YoY Revenue Growth

28.04% Margin Stability

445.79K Total Units Sold

Core Visuals:

Monthly Revenue Comparison (2025 vs 2024)

Revenue Distribution by Pharmacy Type (Urban/Suburban/Rural)

Geographic Revenue Map (Country > Region > City)

Store Size Revenue Contribution

Drilldown Revenue by Location

Business Impact:

This page enables leadership to quickly assess overall performance health, regional contribution, and growth acceleration patterns.

🏬 Page 2: Operational Efficiency & Store Performance
Purpose:

Analyze transaction behavior, pricing strategy, and store-level efficiency.

Key KPIs:

Total Sales Count

Revenue YoY Growth %

Average Cost per Unit

Average Selling Price

Average Revenue per Sale

Core Analysis:

Promotion Profitability (Revenue vs Margin Impact)

Store Opening Trend (Expansion Velocity)

Pharmacy-Level Performance Scorecard

Store Profitability Positioning Matrix (High Revenue vs High Margin)

Key Findings:

Strong sales velocity growth

Stable pricing realization

Clear differentiation between high-revenue and high-margin stores

Identifiable promotion-driven margin compression

Business Impact:

This page helps operational managers optimize store productivity, pricing discipline, and expansion strategy.

📦 Page 3: Product & Promotion Strategy Performance
Purpose:

Evaluate product mix dynamics and promotional effectiveness.

Key KPIs:

Promo Impact on Margin

Promo Units Share %

Revenue at Risk %

Promotion Lift

Generic vs Branded Margin Gap

Core Analysis:

Revenue & Margin Contribution by Category > Brand > SKU

Generic vs Branded Revenue Split

Revenue Dependency on Promotions

Pack Size Volume & Contribution Analysis

Detailed Product Profitability Matrix

Strategic Insights:

Promotions show negative lift (-2.39%), reducing volume efficiency

Revenue concentration risk in discounted SKUs

Margin gap between branded and generic products

Pack size mix reveals volume concentration patterns

Business Impact:

Supports better promotion structuring, product assortment decisions, and pricing optimization strategies.
